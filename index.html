Seoyoung Lee

I am a Master's student at Seoul National University Visual Information Processing Lab, advised by Prof. Joonseok Lee. My research interest lies in the areas of computer vision and machine learning.

Previously, I received Bachelor's degrees in Statistics and Business Administration at Seoul National University, where I worked with Prof. Taesung Park and Prof. Iksoo Huh on statistical meta-analysis at Bioinformatics and Biostatistics Lab.
